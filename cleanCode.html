비동기 데이터 패칭 패턴
컴포넌트에서 외부 데이터를 패칭해 사용하는 경우 아래와 같은 패턴을 많이 사용합니다.

Suspense is similar to ErrorBoundary, But instead of catching the error, it is catching Promise that is thrown from the children, render fallback while the promise is pending, and unblock the children when the promise is resolved.

The top-level component renders three components: A component that will catch errors from anything below it and toggle its UI accordingly. : A component that detect loading states from anything below it and toggle its UI accordingly
